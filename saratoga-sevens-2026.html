§ 03

Questions

What am I buying, exactly?
Digital downloads are full-resolution, unwatermarked files delivered instantly by email — yours to print, post, and share. Prints and canvases are produced by a professional photo lab and shipped directly to your door.
Can I share photos I purchase?
Yes. Every purchase includes a personal-use license — post them, print them, send them to teammates. Commercial use (ads, merchandise, sponsorship material) needs a separate license; just email me.
How long will the gallery stay up?
Through January 2027. After that the gallery comes down — grab what you want before the season ends.
I saw you shooting my match but can’t find a photo of me.
With 39 teams across five simultaneous fields, I couldn’t capture every player in every match — but email me before assuming a shot doesn’t exist. I archive everything, including frames that didn’t make the gallery cut.
Can you shoot my team, club, or event?
That’s the job. Tournament coverage, single matches, and season packages — details on the main site, or email hello@jamesmichelphotography.com.

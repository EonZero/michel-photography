§ 03 — About

Closer than
the touchline.

I'm a rugby photographer based in the Northeast. I played scrumhalf for four years and flanker for four more before I started shooting, and I still read the pitch the same way.

What I'm after is the moment just before the moment — the nine's eyes flicking to the blindside, a lock's hand rising above the pack, the half-second a winger decides he's going inside. Rugby rewards patience. So does a long lens.

Men's and women's sides, 7s and 15s. Mostly club through open division, mostly in the Northeast.
